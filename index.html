About This Calculator

This GPA calculator is specifically designed for Grade 12 Science stream students under the National Examination Board (NEB) of Nepal. It accurately computes your GPA based on the official NEB grading system, taking into account both theory and practical components.

The calculator supports all Science stream subjects including Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, English, and Nepali with their respective credit hours.

  • 100% accurate NEB grading system
  • Both theory and practical components
  • Correct credit hour calculations
  • Detailed marksheet view
  • Printable results

GPA Calculation Formula

GPA = (∑(Credit Hour × Grade Point)) / (Total Credit Hours)

Final Grade = Weighted average of Theory and Practical components

NEB Grading System

Grade Grade Point Percentage Range Description
A+ 4.0 90-100% Outstanding
A 3.6 80-89% Excellent
B+ 3.2 70-79% Very Good
B 2.8 60-69% Good
C+ 2.4 50-59% Above Average
C 2.0 40-49% Average
D 1.6 30-39% Below Average
NG 0.0 Below 30% Non-Graded

Note: The final grade is calculated based on weighted average of theory (75%) and practical (25%) components, except for Computer Science which has 50% theory and 50% practical.
